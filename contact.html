Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my chimney cleaned?

We recommend annual chimney cleaning and inspection, typically before the heating season begins. If you use your fireplace frequently, you may need more frequent cleanings.

How long does a chimney cleaning take?

A standard chimney cleaning typically takes 45 minutes to 1.5 hours, depending on the condition of the chimney and accessibility.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes! We provide 24/7 emergency chimney services for urgent situations such as chimney fires, structural damage, or dangerous blockages.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. All our technicians are fully certified, licensed, and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance for your protection.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards. Payment is due upon completion of service.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we offer free estimates for all chimney repair and installation projects. Contact us to schedule an assessment.
